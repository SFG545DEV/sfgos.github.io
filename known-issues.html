Current Issues with SFGOS

While we work hard to provide the best experience, some issues have been discovered in the process. Below is a list of known issues along with workarounds where available.

1. Bootloader

The rEFInd bootloader is being used as a temporary workaround as grub is not working at the moment in the 1.0 beta.

2. Calamares Packages

We are aware of a issue where calamares does not remove installation packages in the 1.0 Beta.
